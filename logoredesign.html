Logo Redesign

Here is my submission for a logo redesign contest for the student council at my college. I went for a simple but representative approach. The building in the middle is Science Hall, one of the core buildings of CCSF. I enjoyed brainstorming the redesign, planning it on Krita, and creating the new logo on Adobe Illustrator.
